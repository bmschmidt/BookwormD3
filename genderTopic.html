Gendered Language in Teacher Reviews

This chart shows the usage rates of different LDA topics used to describe male and female teachers in about 3 million reviews from RateMyProfessor.com. You can choose any individual topic from the dropdown, and limit to just positive (4 or 5) reviews.

I haven't looked at this much yet, but it seems that the topics may be actually less gendered in many was than the individual words. One feature that will make this funny is that documents are individual reviews, and each professor often has dozens of reviews; so there will be some odd internal-clustering effects.
